What is Flaties ?

Flaties is a solution imaginated by 10 ESSEC students. 4 of them were sharing a flat, and noticed several MAJOR issues. Flaties is an app made for making flatsharing EASY. It aims at improving communication, organization, and financial aspects.

How can I get Flaties ?

To whom does Flaties direct itself ?

We want to offer our solution directly to residences. If you are the director of a residence, contact us ! If you are a flatmmate but you are interested anyway, please leave us a message and we will reach your administrators for you!
